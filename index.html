Genesis of ECAS

Civilized men refused to adapt to the environment. Instead, they adapted their environment to suit them. So they built cities, infrastructure, machinery and developed culture & traditions to make their life easy. The more they improved their surroundings to make their life easier, the more complicated it became. They didn’t know when to stop and therefore, they depleted the environmental resources to develop an artificial environment, which has degraded the quality of life. All humans were born naked, helpless and ignorant. They found their way through trial and error. Gradually, they discovered that they had a mind that discovered principles, which could not be comprehended by the brains of any other creature. When the precursors of human race started understanding these principles, they began by building their habitats and developing with whatever was available to them by Mother Earth through their primitive skills and techniques for survival.

“If we want to begin our position-fixing aboard our spaceship earth we must first acknowledge that the abundance of immediately consumable, obviously desirable or utterly essential resources have been sufficient until now, to allow us to carry on despite our ignorance. This cushion-for-error of humanity’s survival and growth up to now was apparently provided just as a fetus inside an egg is provided with liquid nutrient to develop it to a certain point.”

Birth of ECAS

ECAS believes that humanity shapes our environment at first place, rather than environment shaping our behavioral patterns. Human culture is a symptom of our interaction with the physical world, which has changed rapidly over periods measured in decades or centuries. Being aware of our micro-cultures, such as our interaction with products around us, could bring positive impact to achieve Environmental Conscious Lifestyle.

Meaning of ECAS

ECAS, the name conveys a lot about vision and purpose of the organization. The word ECAS is pronounced as ek-aas, which has origin from Devnagri script and is made from two syllables, which are ek + aas. Ek means one, whereas Aas means longing / desire / faith / shelter / refuge. Therefore, holistic translation of ECAS means ONENESS, which imparts a message of togetherness. The word ‘ECAS’ is simple, memorable, and could be spoken with an effortless exhale of breath. ECAS is not merely any place or organization, but a feeling of oneness which invites all beings to come together with harmony and grow in the process of attaining peace.

Concept of the Logo

The concept of the logo is CIRCLE OF LIFE. ECAS is written in a manner which creates the illusion of closing the circular alphabetical loop (Environment Cycle). While the punch line “ecological living!” guides the approach towards finishing the cycle. The mirrored “C” reflects the stereotypical perception of people towards life, at the same time unconsciously make them conscious to unlearn.

Story of Pencil

ZERO pencil was designed and created to spread awareness of sustainable lifestyle solutions. It is a direct and creative solution to an environmental concern. ZERO Pencil is a rustic wooden twig pencil with seed, which can be recycled after its usage. While one end of the pencil is used for writing, the other end of the pencil holds a seed which will grow into a plant when planted to shed more twigs for pencils.
